Turn your data to value | Make data driven Decisions

Where to start

Getting answers from a data starts with asking the right question. A more detailed question yields actionable inside from the data you use. Suppose you are not sure what to ask, we can start the conversation with the following:

  • This is question I want answered, here is the data.
  • I have the data and know the problem I want to address, but not sure the question to ask.
  • I want to answer this question, but I don’t have the data.
  • This is my objective, not sure what question to ask, and not sure where to get the data?
  • I want to build interactive geospatial maps and blend it with this data

How it works

To get questions answered from data, it is imperative to prepare a high quality targeted data that addresses missing values, improved with feature engineering and adhere to ‘tidy’ principles.

  • Import the data from Excel, a database or any other source
  • Clean and tidy the data
  • Reshape and Transform data
  • Blend and summarize data
  • Generate visualization graphics with ggplot2
  • Work with your SME to refine and model data
  • Primarily, I use R statical pogromming language and tidyverse principles

What I deliver to you

The result of the data analysis can be delivered as reproducible document, graphic plot, dashboard or an algorithm that you can embed in your system. Depending on the project, these are some of the deliverables you will get:

  • Reproducible report including the findings and recommendations
  • Reviewing the report with you
  • Additional answers to questions that come up when analyzing the data
  • Visualization plots generated with ggplot2
  • Dashboard or Web application
  • Geospatial maps (static or interactive)
  • Training to you or your staff (in person or remote)
